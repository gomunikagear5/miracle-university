FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are refunds available?▼
Refunds are not offered. However, in cases of unavoidable circumstances, transfer to the next term is possible. Please contact us for details.
Is installment payment possible?▼
Yes, we accept consultations for installment payments. Please inquire via the application form or contact us.
Are sessions recorded?▼
Sessions are not recorded. Nao’s sessions are live and tuned to the energy of the exact moment. We value real-time participation.
Is English available?▼
The current program is primarily in Japanese. An English version is planned for the future.
What is the difference from 1:1 sessions?▼
1:1 sessions are completely private and deep. Miracle University is a group experience designed as an entry point to learn Nao’s cosmic wisdom together with others.
